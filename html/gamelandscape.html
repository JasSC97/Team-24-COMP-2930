GOT NEXT

Description

Soccer: A soccer game requires at least 10 players and at most 22 players. A match consists of two 45 minutes halves with 15 minutes rest period in between. The host game needs to bring a ball

Basketball: A basketball game requires at least 2 players and at most 10 players. A game is split up into 4 twelve minutes quarters. In between the 2nd and 3rd quarter is a 15 minutes half time interval. The host needs to bring a ball.

Tennis: A tennis game requires at least 2 players and at most 4 players. The tennis match can be played by either one player on each side – a singles match – or two players on each side- a doubles match. A game includes 3 sets for women or 5 sets for men. The host game needs to bring a ball and a racket. Other players need to bring their own rackets.
